    Jerusalem City

    Jerusalem, the largest city in occupied historical Palestine in terms of area and population, and the most important religiously and economically. It is known by other names in the Arabic language, such as: Bayt Al-Maqdis, Al-Quds Al-Sharif, the first of the two Qiblas, and in the Bible as Jerusalem, and Israel officially calls it: Jerusalem Al-Quds.

    Jericho City

    Jericho is an ancient historic Palestinian city located in the West Bank near the Jordan River and north of the Dead Sea, dating back to 10,000 BC. It is the capital of Jericho Governorate and is the oldest city in history.

    Gaza City

    Gaza is a Palestinian coastal city, and the largest city in the Gaza Strip is located in its north, at the southern tip of the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea.

    Safed City

    Safed is one of the historical cities of Palestine. The city is located in the Galilee region. Today, it is in the northern region of Israel. It is also considered one of the country's highest cities, about 900 meters above sea level.

    Read More

    Jafa City

    Jaffa is one of the oldest and most important historical cities in Palestine. Today it is located within the Israeli municipality of "Tel Aviv - Jaffa", on the eastern coast of the Mediterranean Sea - according to the Israeli administrative division. It is 55 km from Jerusalem to the west.
